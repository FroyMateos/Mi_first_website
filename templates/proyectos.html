Abrictosaurus was a small dinosaur that lived in the early Jurassic period, between about 199 and 196 million years ago. Abrictosaurus was quite small. It was about 1.2 meters long, and weighed only 45 kilograms.

Diplodocus

Diplodocus lived in the Jurassic period, 155 to 145 million years ago. Diplodocus was usually around 25.9 meters long, although some specimens were as long as 30.5 meters. Most of its length was taken up its 7.3 meters neck, and its 14 meters tail. Its body was only about 4 meters long, and its head was comparatively tiny, just 60 centimeters long.

Tyrannosaurus

Tyrannosaurus rex lived during late Cretaceous period, about 67 to 65 million years ago. It was one of the last dinosaurs, and probably went extinct during the Cretaceous-Tertiary mass extinction. It turns out that the undisputed king of the dinosaurs, Tyrannosaurus rex, didn't just eat other dinosaurs but also each other.
